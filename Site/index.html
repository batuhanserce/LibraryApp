Library App

Can't find the right place to read a book? or Don't want to carry your books with you?
Here is an application just for you, with free and unlimited resources.

Features

No Payment

It is a completely free and open source platform. just register and enjoy reading

Mobile Friendly

It is enough to have a device that can connect to the internet wherever and whenever you want. Of course it's also compatible for mobile.

Social Network

You can see what people like and review the most popular trending books.

Instant access to millions of books.
